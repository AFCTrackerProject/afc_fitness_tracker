About Us

At AFC Fitness, we're passionate about helping you achieve your health and fitness goals, no matter your level of experience or fitness background. Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or you're a seasoned athlete, we're here to support and inspire you every step of the way.

Our Mission

Our mission at AFC Fitness is simple: to empower individuals to lead healthier, happier lives through fitness, nutrition, and community. We believe that everyone deserves access to the resources and support they need to reach their full potential, both physically and mentally.

What We Offer

  • Nutrition Tracking: Set macro goals and track progress.
  • Community Support: Connect with fellow fitness enthusiasts on a public forum.
  • Fitness Guidance: Get advice from a virtual assistant chatbot.
  • Workout Resources: Access extensive workouts lists for every muscle group.
  • Fitness Finder: Discover nearby places to excercise and eat well.
